Basic Exercise For Beginners..

Bridge


The bridge exercise, also known as a bridge pose or hip raise, offers several benefits for the body: Strengthens the core muscles: The bridge exercise primarily targets the glutes, lower back, and abdominal muscles, helping to improve overall core strength. Improves hip stability: By engaging the muscles around the hips and pelvis, the bridge exercise can help improve hip stability and reduce the risk of injuries. Enhances lower body strength: This exercise also engages the hamstrings, quadriceps, and calves, helping to strengthen the lower body muscles. Improves posture: Bridge exercises can help improve posture by strengthening the muscles that support the spine and pelvis.

Knee Pushups


Knee pushups, also known as modified pushups, offer a variety of benefits, especially for individuals who are working on improving their upper body strength or are unable to perform full standard pushups. Here are some benefits of knee pushups: Strengthens chest, shoulders, and arms: Knee pushups target the chest, shoulders, and arms, helping to build strength in these upper body muscles. Engages core muscles: While performing knee pushups, the core muscles are engaged to stabilize the body, leading to improved core strength. Reduces wrist strain: Knee pushups can be an excellent alternative for individuals who experience wrist pain or strain during regular pushups, as they put less stress on the wrists.

Wall Squat


Wall squats, also known as wall sits, are a beneficial lower body exercise that offers several advantages for building strength and endurance. Here are some benefits of wall squats: Strengthens the quadriceps: Wall squats primarily target the quadriceps, the large muscles at the front of the thighs. By holding a static squat position against the wall, these muscles are engaged and strengthened. Engages the glutes and hamstrings: Wall squats also engage the glutes (buttocks) and hamstrings (back of the thighs), helping to improve overall lower body strength. Improves endurance: Holding a wall squat position for an extended period can help improve muscular endurance in the lower body, which can be beneficial for various physical activities and sports.
